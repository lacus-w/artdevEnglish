# Concept Art English: Survival & Influence in AAA Pipelines

> **Subtitle:** A Concept Artist's Field Guide to Workplace English  
> **Audience:** Strict Concept Artists (Character / Environment / Prop)  
> **Level:** Intermediate–Advanced (production-focused, not academic)

---

## About This Book

This textbook is **not** about learning English. It's about **using English as a design tool** in high-pressure, professional concept art environments.

Every chapter is built around real AAA / film / outsource production scenarios. You won't find "How are you?" here. Instead, you'll learn how to:

- Decode Art Director feedback without losing your mind
- Navigate sprint planning meetings with confidence
- Write Jira comments and emails that protect your scope
- Build a professional presence in the global industry

## How to Use This Book

1. **Read scenario → practice phrases → do the homework.**  
   Each chapter follows the same structure:  
   *Scenario → Survival Phrases → Subtext Decoder → Roleplay → Common Mistakes → Pipeline Snapshot → Homework → Lead's Note*

2. **Don't just memorize.**  
   The phrases are starting points. Adapt them to your own voice and studio culture.

3. **Revisit after 3 months.**  
   You'll understand the subtext on a deeper level once you've lived through a few production cycles.
